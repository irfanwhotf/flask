Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to AI Resume Builder ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. Data We Collect

When you use our AI Resume & Cover Letter Generator, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details you provide when creating a resume or cover letter.
  • Professional Information: Work experience, education history, skills, and other professional details you provide for your resume or cover letter.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and other diagnostic data.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience on our site. See our Cookie Policy section for more details.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service, including generating resumes and cover letters based on your input.
  • To improve our website and services based on your feedback and usage patterns.
  • To display personalized content and advertisements through Google AdSense.
  • To communicate with you about updates, new features, or support requests.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or potential violations of our terms of service.

4. Data Storage and Security

We do not permanently store your personal information or resume/cover letter content beyond your current session. Your data is only used temporarily to generate your documents and is not saved after you leave the site.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Cookie Policy

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the operation of our website, such as session management.
  • Analytical Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements through Google AdSense based on your interests.

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Please note that removing or blocking cookies may impact your experience on our website.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google may use cookies to personalize ads based on your browsing behavior. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and improve our service. You can learn more about how Google Analytics collects and processes data at https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.
  • Google Gemini AI: We use Google's Gemini AI to generate resume and cover letter content. Your input data is processed according to Google's AI principles and privacy policies.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • The right to rectification if your information is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • The right to object to our processing of your personal data.
  • The right to request restriction of processing of your personal data.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time where we relied on your consent to process your personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
